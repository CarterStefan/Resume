About Me

What do I do?

As someone who always enjoys taking on new challenges and learning new skills, I did not want to let the recent events in the world go by without coming out of it with new knowledge and a new focus for the rest of my life. I am currently part way through my studies with the Code Institute, studying towards a diploma in Full Stack Web Development.

I am hoping to now get my foot in the door, and kick start my career using the skills I am learning through the Code Institute.

Why hire me?

I am a passionate person always wanting to improve myself and the team and company around me, with the mindset that I work with the company, not for the company.
