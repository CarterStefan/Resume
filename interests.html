About Me

I have held various roles over the years, doing a wide variety of work. Once the pandemic hit at the begining of this year, I felt it was time to learn a new skill and put myself on the path to a succesful career in a role I enjoy. I decided to do my studying through the Code Institute, and gain a diploma in Full Stack web development.

I started to gain an intrest in web development from my time as a project co-ordinator. Leading the team of developers in solving technical bugs in the system, or new features, I was always keen to learn what went wrong, as well as how it went wrong. This gave me a basic understanding of HTML and CSS as well as how a back end system works and links together.

After taking some time out of my career for myself, and finding myself with more spare time on my hands due to the pandemic, I wanted to come out of it with a new skill set and focus in my life. I feel now I am ready to start this new career in web development, and would greatly appreciate the opportunity.
